About Codag

Compound Directed Acyclic Graphs

As agentic systems become more complex—multi-agent workflows, tool orchestration, RAG pipelines—understanding what your AI is actually doing becomes critical. Codag gives you real-time visibility into the code that Claude Code, Cursor, and other AI assistants are generating.

See exactly how agents coordinate, where LLM calls happen, and how data flows through your system. Debug multi-agent workflows visually. The missing piece of the agentic coding experience.
